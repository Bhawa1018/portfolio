Multitasking Bed

This project focuses on developing an intelligent bed system that improves patient care through the use of AI and sensor technology. The bed can monitor a patient's vital signs, adjust itself for comfort, and alert healthcare providers to any sudden changes in the patient's condition.

Technologies used: AI, IoT, Sensor Technology, Healthcare Systems
